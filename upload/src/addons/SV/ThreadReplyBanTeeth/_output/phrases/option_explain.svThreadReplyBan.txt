Prevent someone who has been thread reply banned from editing various thread settings in that thread. covers:<ul>
<li>Editing the thread</li>
<li>Deleting the thread</li>
<li>Lock/unlock</li>
<li>Stick/unstick thread</li>
<li>Create polls</li>
<li>Post friction settings (<i>SV/PostFriction</i> add-on)</li>
<li>Thread Collaboration settings (<i>SV/CollaborativeThreads</i> add-on)</li>
</ul>
